HANDCRAFTED, ALL-WEATHER, CONCRETEWARE

small batch planters, lamps, and other household items

Combining traditional concrete casting with new technologies

Computer Modeling

Starting with CAD software, ideas are brought into digital space, from there a design can be modified until a final digital representation is chosen and a digital mold is created.

3D Printing

Utilizing adaptive manufacturing technology and new high-strength and high-temperature materials, ready-to-cast physical molds are created from their digital counterparts.

Concrete Casting

A traditional medium takes on new forms when combined with new technologies. Exciting new designs previously unattainable with concrete casting can now be realized.
